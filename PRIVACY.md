## What this is

This policy describes how the operator of this Film Club instance ("we", "us")
handles information when you use it.

## Information we collect

- **Account information** you provide: username, email address, password (stored
  only as a salted hash — never in plain text), and any optional profile details
  (display name, bio, avatar URL, region).
- **Content you create:** diary entries, ratings, reviews, lists, watchlist
  items, follows, and likes.
- **Imported data:** if you import a Letterboxd CSV, the entries it contains.
- **Technical data:** standard server logs (such as IP address, request time,
  and user agent) generated when your browser contacts the server.

We do **not** serve ads, and we do not sell your personal information.

- **Email.** If configured, we use an SMTP provider to send transactional email
  (verification and password-reset messages). [Name your provider here.]

  technical data as part of delivering the service.

## How we use information

- To operate your account and provide the app's features.
- To send transactional email you request or that's required for your account
  (verification, password reset).
- To secure the service and diagnose problems.

## Cookies and local storage

Authentication uses a token stored in your browser's local storage. We do not
use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.
